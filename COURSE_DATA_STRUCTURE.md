# Course Data Structure - 10 Bharatanatyam Levels

## Overview
The academy offers 10 structured courses progressing from beginner basics to professional Arangetram preparation.

## Course List

### 1. Angikam Basics
- **ID**: `angikam-basics`
- **Age Group**: Kids (5-7 years)
- **Price**: ₹2,500
- **Duration**: 3 months
- **Level**: Beginner
- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Mudras**: 5 basic mudras (Pataka, Tripataka, Ardhapataka, Kartarimukha, Mayura)
- **Adavus**: Tatta Adavu (8 counts), Natta Adavu (4 counts), Basic postures
- **Theory**: Introduction to Bharatanatyam, History, Basic terminology, Tala, Carnatic music intro

### 2. Adavu Module 1
- **ID**: `adavu-module-1`
- **Age Group**: Kids (5-7 years)
- **Price**: ₹3,000
- **Duration**: 4 months
- **Level**: Beginner
- **Prerequisites**: Angikam Basics or equivalent
- **Mudras**: 5 additional mudras (Ardhachandra, Arala, Shukatunda, Mushti, Shikhara)
- **Adavus**: Complete Tatta & Natta sets, Kuditta Mettu, Mandi (basic), Sarikal
- **Theory**: Detailed Tatta Adavu study, Adi Tala, Hand-foot coordination, Basic Abhinaya

### 3. Adavu Module 2
- **ID**: `adavu-module-2`
- **Age Group**: Juniors (8-10 years)
- **Price**: ₹3,500
- **Duration**: 4 months
- **Level**: Intermediate
- **Prerequisites**: Adavu Module 1
- **Mudras**: 5 advanced mudras (Kapitha, Katakamukha, Suchi, Chandrakala, Padmakosha)
- **Adavus**: Advanced Kuditta Mettu, Complete Mandi set, Advanced Sarikal, Tirmanam, Jati
- **Theory**: Advanced rhythm patterns, Different Talas, Jatis introduction, Body alignment

### 4. Shloka & Mudra Theory
- **ID**: `shloka-mudra-theory`
- **Age Group**: All Ages
- **Price**: ₹2,000
- **Duration**: 2 months
- **Level**: Theory
- **Prerequisites**: None (Can be taken alongside practical classes)
- **Mudras**: All 28 Asamyuta Hastas, All 24 Samyuta Hastas, Deva Hastas, Dashavatara, Navagraha
- **Adavus**: None (Theory only)
- **Theory**: Abhinaya Darpana, Mudra meanings, Shloka recitation, Sanskrit pronunciation, Cultural context

### 5. Alarippu & Jatiswaram
- **ID**: `alarippu-jatiswaram`
- **Age Group**: Juniors (10+ years)
- **Price**: ₹4,000
- **Duration**: 5 months
- **Level**: Intermediate
- **Prerequisites**: Adavu Module 2 or equivalent experience
- **Mudras**: Application of all Asamyuta & Samyuta Hastas, Mudra combinations
- **Adavus**: Complex combinations, Tirmanam patterns, Jati variations, Speed variations
- **Theory**: Alarippu structure, Jatiswaram structure, Swaras, Raga basics, Tala variations

### 6. Shabdam & Basic Abhinaya
- **ID**: `shabdam-abhinaya`
- **Age Group**: Juniors (11-13 years)
- **Price**: ₹4,500
- **Duration**: 6 months
- **Level**: Intermediate-Advanced
- **Prerequisites**: Alarippu & Jatiswaram
- **Mudras**: Abhinaya Mudras, Navarasa Mudras, Contextual usage
- **Adavus**: Abhinaya-oriented patterns, Slow tempo, Transition movements
- **Theory**: Abhinaya introduction, Navarasa (9 emotions), Shabdam structure, Storytelling, Facial expressions, Eye movements

### 7. Varnam Intensive
- **ID**: `varnam-intensive`
- **Age Group**: Adults (14+ years)
- **Price**: ₹6,000
- **Duration**: 8 months
- **Level**: Advanced
- **Prerequisites**: Shabdam & Basic Abhinaya or 3+ years experience
- **Mudras**: Advanced combinations, Complex Abhinaya Mudras, Varnam sequences
- **Adavus**: Complex Jati patterns, Advanced Tirmanam, Speed variations, Music synchronization
- **Theory**: Complete Varnam structure, Pallavi/Anupallavi/Charanam, Sahitya meaning, Raga/Tala deep dive, Performance techniques, Endurance training

### 8. Padam & Ashtapadi
- **ID**: `padam-ashtapadi`
- **Age Group**: Adults (14+ years)
- **Price**: ₹5,500
- **Duration**: 6 months
- **Level**: Advanced
- **Prerequisites**: Varnam Intensive or equivalent advanced training
- **Mudras**: Subtle Abhinaya Mudras, Emotional expression, Devotional gestures
- **Adavus**: Minimal Nritta, maximum Abhinaya, Slow graceful movements, Emotional transitions
- **Theory**: Padam structure & philosophy, Ashtapadi (Gita Govinda), Bhakti Rasa, Sringara Rasa, Subtlety in expression, Poetry interpretation, Cultural context

### 9. Thillana & Mangalam
- **ID**: `thillana-mangalam`
- **Age Group**: Adults (16+ years)
- **Price**: ₹5,000
- **Duration**: 5 months
- **Level**: Senior
- **Prerequisites**: Padam & Ashtapadi or 5+ years experience
- **Mudras**: All mudras in fast sequences, Complex patterns, Devotional Mudras
- **Adavus**: Fast-paced complex Adavus, Thillana Jati patterns, Technical precision, Performance-ready
- **Theory**: Thillana structure, Mangalam conclusion, Performance presentation, Stage presence, Complete Margam understanding

### 10. Arangetram Prep
- **ID**: `arangetram-prep`
- **Age Group**: Adults (16+ years)
- **Price**: ₹12,000
- **Duration**: 12 months
- **Level**: Elite/Professional
- **Prerequisites**: Completion of all previous levels or 6+ years intensive training
- **Mudras**: Mastery of all Mudras, Performance-ready sequences, Advanced Abhinaya
- **Adavus**: Complete Margam choreography, Stamina training, Perfection of all Adavus, Performance-level execution
- **Theory**: Complete Margam, Performance techniques, Music coordination, Costume & makeup, Stage management, Cultural etiquette, Professional presentation, Arangetram planning

## Data Model (TypeScript Interface)

```typescript
interface Course {
  id: string;
  name: string;
  description: string;
  ageGroup: 'Kids' | 'Juniors' | 'Adults' | 'All Ages';
  ageRange: string;
  price: number;
  duration: string;
  level: string;
  syllabus: {
    mudras: string[];
    adavus: string[];
    theory: string[];
  };
  prerequisites?: string;
}
```
